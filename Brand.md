## 2. ACTIVITY: BRAND ARCHETYPES

![image.png](attachment:cb01c779-c8c6-4352-b86b-33c2e710703e:image.png)

Your customer insights reveal a powerful combination.

1. **THE CARETAKER:** This is the brand's heart.
    - **Evidence:** "Empowering people," "removes...anxiety," "your friendly, reliable houseplant mentor," "warm, dependable."
    - **Role:** You protect and nurture your customers, ensuring their success and removing their fears.
2. **THE MAGICIAN:** This is the brand's hand.
    - **Evidence:** "Plant fairy-godmother," "Godsend."
    - **Role:** A "Sage" (which we first thought) simply *gives information*. A "Magician" creates *transformation*. You transform customer "anxiety" into "joy" and "confidence." You transform a complex, failing hobby into a simple, successful one. This is a more potent and accurate "Big Idea."

**OUR ARCHETYPE:** **The Nurturing Magician**
